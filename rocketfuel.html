Rocket Fuel
Keeps Your Mac Awake
Rocket Fuel is a small, easy to use app that sits discreetly in your menu bar awaiting your command. A simple click will prevent your Mac from going to sleep or dimming its display.
  • One-click activation
  • Auto launch on startup
  • Deactivates after a preset time
  • Works with dark mode in Yosemite
  • Support for AppleScript
